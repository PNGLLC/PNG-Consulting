The Problem Isn't Your People. It's Your Processes.

Most businesses don't fail from one large mistake. They bleed from dozens of small, invisible ones — missed calls, manual intake, undocumented scope changes, approval bottlenecks, and compliance gaps that compound over months.

  • Change orders and scope shifts that never make it into the contract
  • Compliance documentation tracked on spreadsheets and memory
  • Admin bottlenecks that delay approvals, billing, and handoffs
  • Staff spending 10+ hours a week on tasks that should take two
